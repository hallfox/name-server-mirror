Somehow, perhaps by using your trusty Space-Time Vortex
Manipulator that you keep on your wrist at all times,
you find your way into this room.

It's a small janitorial closet. In it you find twelve sticks
of dynamite and a corroded green key. You figure to yourself the
key will be more useful to you, so you pick it up and drop it in
your trenchcoat pocket

Too bad you didn't use your Vortex Manipulator to get you out
of this place. You only had enough energy to make one trip, and
the only place you can go now is back where you came from.
